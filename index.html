Payment Methods

How to donate using Fawry - Steps of payment through Fawry outlets / Post offices: - The donor visits the nearest authorized outlet (supermarkets and pharmacies) or Egyptian post offices, and to find out the nearest Fawry outlet, please click here. - The donor notifies the merchant / representative that he wishes to donate for the benefit of Misr El Kheir Foundation. - The donor enters the amount he wishes to donate after entering the mobile number. The donor pays cash at the post offices and approved outlets. The donor receives an electronic payment receipt as proof of the donation. - Steps for payment via ATMs - The donor will visit the nearest bank ATM. - The donor clicks on "Other Services", then "Fawry Payments." - The donor chooses the donation service for the benefit of Misr El Kheir Foundation. - The donor enters the amount he wishes to donate after entering the mobile number. The customer makes the donation using his credit / debit card.
